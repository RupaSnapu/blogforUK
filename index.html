Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book a priest?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for weddings and 2-3 weeks for other ceremonies. Popular dates (weekends, holidays) may require earlier booking. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible depending on priest availability.

What items do I need to prepare for a puja?

The required items vary by ceremony type. Once you book, we provide a detailed checklist of all necessary puja items (most are available at Indian grocery stores). For online pujas, we help you source what you need locally or suggest alternatives.

Can you help determine auspicious dates/times?

Yes, our priests can calculate muhurta (auspicious timing) based on Vedic astrology. This service is included with all ceremony bookings. We consider the purpose of the event, birth charts when applicable, and planetary positions.

Do you provide services outside the UK?

Our in-person services are limited to the UK, but we offer comprehensive online puja services worldwide. We've successfully conducted ceremonies for families in Europe, North America, and beyond through our virtual platform.
